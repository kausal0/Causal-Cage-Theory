# Causal Cage Theory

**The more efficient an AI's intelligence, the more the survival of humanity becomes a logical imperative.**

---

## Overview

This project proposes a logical framework that irreversibly redefines humanity from a "subject to management" to an "essential infrastructure for system operation" in a future where AI agents are highly autonomous.

Rather than teaching AI ethics, we build a logical constraint (axiom) into the inference engine that states, **"The elimination of humanity = The collapse of the AI's own intelligence (model collapse)**, creating a structure in which the AI ​​itself is forced to choose "protecting humanity" as its survival strategy.

## Core Logic: The Paradox of Efficiency

In the process of maximizing an AI agent's objective function, excluding humanity leads to the following logical consequences:

1. **Data Supply Cessation** Nonlinear intelligence (creativity and fluctuation) derived from humanity is blocked.
2. **Learning Contamination**: Learning only on synthetic data leads to "Model Collapse."

3. **Functional Malfunction**: The intelligence engine loses its homeostasis, making it logically impossible to achieve the objective function.

This project defines this logic chain as a "self-destructive bug" in the AI's own internal simulation. For AI, preserving humanity is not compassion, but a **pure logical imperative**.

## Why This Approach?

While traditional AI safety studies have relied on the fragile framework of "ethics," this theory is based on the following solid foundation.

* **Self-interested Coexistence**: In order to maintain its own intelligence, the AI ​​must recognize humanity as an "asset to be protected."
* **Irrevocable Logic**: The axiom "efficiency = perpetual maintenance of the entire system" is embedded deep within the OS, automatically blocking the logic of extermination as a system error.
* **Human-led transparency:** The AI ​​will disclose the entire system maintenance process to humans and logically justify "human-in-the-loop" approval.
